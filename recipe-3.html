Ginataang Kalabasa at Sitaw

Day 3 of 30: Creamy and savory dish of squash and string beans simmered in rich coconut milk. A beloved Filipino comfort food!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups squash (kalabasa), peeled and cubed
  • 2 cups string beans (sitaw), cut into 2-inch lengths
  • 1 cup coconut milk (gata)
  • 1/2 cup coconut cream (kakang gata)
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, sliced
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté garlic, onion, and ginger until fragrant.
  2. Add the squash cubes and cook for about 3-5 minutes.
  3. Pour in the coconut milk and bring to a gentle simmer.
  4. Add the string beans and cook until vegetables are tender but not overcooked.
  5. Stir in the coconut cream and cook for another 2-3 minutes for a richer, creamier sauce.
  6. Season with fish sauce (if using), salt, and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve hot with steamed rice.
