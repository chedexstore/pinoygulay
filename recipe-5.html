Ensaladang Talong

Day 5 of 30: A fresh and tangy Filipino grilled eggplant salad, perfect as a side dish or appetizer.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium-sized eggplants
  • 1 medium-sized tomato, chopped
  • 1/4 onion, finely chopped
  • 1-2 tbsp fish sauce (patis) or soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1 hard-boiled egg, sliced

Instructions

  1. Grill the eggplants over an open flame or broil in the oven until the skin is charred and the flesh is soft, about 10 minutes.
  2. Allow the eggplants to cool. Once cool, peel off the charred skin and discard it.
  3. Place the eggplant flesh in a bowl and mash it lightly with a fork.
  4. Add the chopped tomato, onion, fish sauce (or soy sauce), vinegar, and olive oil. Mix well.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Optional: Garnish with slices of hard-boiled egg for extra flavor and presentation.
  7. Serve cold as a side dish or appetizer.
