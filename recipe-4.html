Chopsuey

Day 4 of 30: A hearty stir-fry of mixed vegetables in a savory sauce, bringing vibrant color and flavor to your table!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cauliflower florets
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup carrots, sliced thinly
  • 1/2 cup young corn, halved
  • 1/2 cup green beans, cut into 2-inch lengths
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup water or vegetable broth
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce or vegetarian alternative
  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1 tsp cornstarch dissolved in 2 tbsp water (for thickening)

Instructions

  1. Heat oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat.
  2. Sauté garlic and onion until fragrant.
  3. Add carrots, cauliflower, and broccoli. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Add green beans, young corn, and bell pepper. Stir-fry for another 2 minutes.
  5. Pour in water or broth and add oyster sauce. Stir well.
  6. If a thicker sauce is desired, stir in the cornstarch slurry and cook until sauce thickens.
  7. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Serve hot with steamed rice!
